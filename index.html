ADAPTIHIP

Design Endorsements

    Our approach included an overall reduction in weight while maintaining structural stability, Inspired by the trellis bike frame

     

    We removed the overall unnecessary materials in the hip implant by giving it a porous structural build. Even though it's theoretical and still under development.

     

UBISOFT

Find The LAG

 

    Participated in Find the LAG competition at Techfest, IIT Bombay by Ubisoft

     

    The solution we came up with was to extract frame-by-frame images from videos and compute the structural similarity index measure on each frame against the frame when the input is given, we need not change the hyperparameters as feed in the input video, and it outperformed the traditional Background Subtraction Method

     

    Secured 2nd place in the competition and received prize amount worth 40k

Chennai Cybersecurity Hackathon

Pixelated Footage Enhancement

 

Enhancing the pixelated CCTV footage, our approach was based on Res-Auto encoders.

 

High-quality videos were collected from the internet and several additional features were added such as gaussian noise, blur effect, erosion, and dilation to the extracted videos which were used as input and the original image as output

 

Our model attained 98.6 percent SSIM accuracy and helped us secure 3rd place.
